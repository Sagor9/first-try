Let's make a bagage what we have to take with us in traveling. Around 3/4 days hangout. How will we make it from the begining to end.

Tour plan:

  1. Bus
  2. Hotel Booking
  3. Breakfast
  4. Ready for vist and go out
  5. Back hotel at night
  6. Take rest

  8. Evening
  9. Marketing

Sitakunda

We go there at 2018. We travel by train from kamlapur railway station to Sitakunda station. Reached at 6 am and start searching for hotel to stay. We get ready and start after a couple hour from hotel. First day we visit Khoiyachora Waterfall. Start our tour again at next day morning with Chandranath Hill. After lunch we go for Guliakhali sea beach. After that we packed and return to Dhaka again.
